Class destructible

destructible objects represent objects that may be removed from the game at some point, effectively wiping them out of the render/process loop.

This does not represent objects that may be destroyed to conserve memory, not to say that the destruction of these objects won't release any. Undertailor handles most of the garbage collection on its own.

Methods

destructible:isDestroyed() Returns whether or not this destructible has been destroyed.
destructible:destroy() Destroys this destructible.
